DISCOVER THE GAME

"Card Wars" is the beloved, in-universe collectible card game from Adventure Time, which gained legendary status after the famous Season 4 episode where Jake fiercely battles Finn. The real-life version is a two-player, lane-based strategy game where players summon creatures, cast spells, and deploy buildings across four distinct Landscapes. Its most iconic, meme-worthy mechanic is the ability to "Floop the Pig," a unique action a creature can take instead of attacking, often involving a powerful ability or sacrifice. The game's objective is to reduce your opponent's Hit Points from 25 to zero, all while hilariously debating who is the "Cool Guy" and who is the "Dweeb." With its vibrant, imaginative art and easy-to-learn but strategic gameplay, Card Wars successfully captures the quirky, competitive spirit of the show.
